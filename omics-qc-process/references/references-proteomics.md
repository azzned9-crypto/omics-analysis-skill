# 蛋白组学 QC 参考文献

1. Geyer PE, Kulak NA, Pichler G, et al. Plasma Proteome Profiling to assess human health and disease. 作为血浆污染 panel 与血浆样本质控思路的重要参考。
2. Lazar C, Gatto L, Ferro M, et al. Accounting for the multiple natures of missing values in label-free quantitative proteomics data sets to compare imputation strategies. 用于缺失值类型和填补策略的背景说明。
3. Cox J, Hein MY, Luber CA, et al. Accurate proteome-wide label-free quantification by delayed normalization and maximal peptide ratio extraction. 作为 label-free 定量与标准化流程参考。
4. Tyanova S, Temu T, Cox J. The MaxQuant computational platform for mass spectrometry-based shotgun proteomics. 作为 MaxQuant 风格蛋白组数据结构参考。
5. Callister SJ, Barry RC, Adkins JN, et al. Normalization approaches for removing systematic biases associated with mass spectrometry and label-free proteomics. 作为标准化方法比较参考。
6. Zhang X, Smits AH, van Tilburg GB, et al. Proteome-wide identification of ubiquitin interactions using UbIA-MS. 作为蛋白组矩阵质控与批次考虑的补充参考。
